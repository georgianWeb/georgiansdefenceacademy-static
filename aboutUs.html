Georgians Defence Academy was established on 1st April 2013 with a passion to motivate and guide the candidates in realising their dream of becoming an officer in the Armed Forces. In the last 8 years this institution has proved its credentials by giving best results in NDA/CDS written and SSB. Earlier it was named as Georgians Academy for Professional Studies(GAPS), but WEF 1st April 2023 its name has been changed to Georgians Defence Academy. Today we are considered the best institution for NDA/CDS written and SSB guidance in Delhi/NCR and one of the best institutions in India for Written and SSB preparation.


GDA is a dream project of Like-minded people who are alumni of "King George's Royal Indian Military College" (KGRIMC) presently known as Rastriya Military School, Ex Armed Forces Officers and Educationists. The sole aim of establishing this institution is to provide a platform to the students of Rastriya Military Schools, Sainik Schools, KV and Wards of Ex- army personnel where they can cherish their dream of wearing uniform and serve the country and the elite Defence Forces.


Our institute caters for the economically weaker section candidates by charging minimum fees from them and provide quality education and best opportunity to achieve success in written test and SSB. We believe that quality education and proper guidance is the basic right of every youngster. No youngster should be deprived of it just because he/she doesn't have resources to realise his/her dream.


Since April 2013 we have taken small strides towards realising bigger dreams. With three recommendations in 2013, seven in 2014, twelve in 2015, eighteen in 2016, twenty-seven in 2017, twenty-seven in 2018, forty-eight in 2019, seventy in 2020, fifty-four in 2021 and sixty- seven in 2022, we have given more than 340 recommendations in SSB for various entries of Army, Navy and Airforce. Georgians Defence Academy feels proud in sharing that it has been able to transform the personality of thousands of students and contribute towards Nation building through these youngsters and budding scholar warriors.
